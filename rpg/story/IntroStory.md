# The Ashen Covenant — Intro Story Hook

## Opening Narration

The village of Duskholm has not seen sunlight in thirty days.

Not since the night the bells rang backwards — low to high, as if tolling in reverse — and the old priest was found at the altar, kneeling, facing the wrong direction, stone cold and smiling.

They say the darkness began in the Mirewald, the rotting forest that wraps around Duskholm like a fist. That something ancient stirred in its depths when the comet passed overhead. That the soil itself has soured — crops blacken before they break ground, livestock die with their eyes wide open, and children wake at midnight whispering names no one taught them.

You are a stranger passing through. You had no intention of staying.

But the gates won't open.

And last night, someone slipped a note under your door at the inn. It bore no signature — only a wax seal pressed with a symbol you've seen before, carved into the ruins of a place you'd rather forget.

Three words:

*"You were summoned."*

---

## Core Themes

- Corruption spreading from a central source (the Mirewald)
- A mystery with a personal connection to the player
- A town under siege that may not want to be saved
- An ancient power that predates the Church
