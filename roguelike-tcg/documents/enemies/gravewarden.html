The Gravewarden

It was built to guard something — no one living remembers what. Whatever it protected is long gone. The Gravewarden remains, and it has grown very tired of intruders.

Undead Boss Sequential AI Holy Weakness Three-Phase Summoner
Description

It was built to guard something — no one living remembers what. The Gravewarden is ancient beyond reckoning, its bones fused with stone and rune-carved iron. It does not move quickly. It does not need to.

The fight unfolds in three distinct stages. The Gravewarden begins measured and methodical. When it cracks, runes ignite and everything hits harder. At near death it stops holding back entirely — desperate, wild, and more dangerous than at full strength. Throughout all three phases it calls skeletons from the earth, and each summon extends the encounter further.

Design Notes
Final boss. Sequential pattern is readable but damage output climbs steeply across phases. Key questions: can the player out-damage the summons, clear block before Crush, and close out before Phase 3 buffs stack? Bone Storm is the panic button — it hurts the Warden to hurt you.
