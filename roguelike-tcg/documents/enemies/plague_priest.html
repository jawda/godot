Description

A cleric who answered a different call. Whatever faith sustained him in life has curdled into something that festers and spreads. The Plague Priest operates as a status machine first — weakening and softening the player before damage becomes decisive.

In his first phase he is measured: he will curse, weaken, and occasionally strike before retreating behind his shield. At 50% HP he tears open his own wounds, entering a desperate second phase where everything hits harder and his Burn stacks begin to define the fight.
