Description

A scavenger grown fat on buried things. It has learned that the living are easier to take from than the dead. Alone, manageable. In a pack, genuinely dangerous.

Its attacks are minor individually, but the Fester bite leaves a lingering Weak debuff that compounds with everything else you're already dealing with. A rat that gets a Fester in early enough will define the shape of the fight.
