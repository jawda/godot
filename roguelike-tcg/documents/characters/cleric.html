The Cleric

Faith is not a shield — it is a weapon.

Biography

Once an ordained healer within the Order of the Pale Flame, the Cleric departed the temple when the hierarchy chose silence over action — allowing the undead sickness spreading through the lower cities to go unanswered. What the Order called patience, the Cleric called complicity.

They carry their faith not as doctrine but as discipline: a set of principles hammered sharp against the years. Where other wanderers fight to survive, the Cleric fights to purge — and endures punishment that would break anyone without their particular relationship with pain.

Class Mechanics
Holy Damage
Holy attacks deal double damage to undead enemies. The Cleric's basic strikes carry this damage type, making them uniquely effective in corrupted regions.
Block Stacking
The Cleric builds mitigation through layered defence rather than evasion. Blessings and Quirks compound block across turns, rewarding a patient, deliberate playstyle.
Life Economy
Several cards scale off current HP or total life gained in combat. Healing is not just survival — it fuels damage and block. Faith increases all HP restored.
